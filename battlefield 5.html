About the Game

Battlefield 5 is a first-person shooter video game developed by EA DICE and published by Electronic Arts. It is the sixteenth installment in the Battlefield series.

The game is set in World War II and features large maps, teamwork-focused gameplay, and a variety of weapons and vehicles.
